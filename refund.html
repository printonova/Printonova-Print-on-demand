Refund & Return Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

At Printonova, customer satisfaction is important to us. Since most of our products are custom-made and personalized, our refund and return policy is outlined below.

Customized Products

Personalized and custom-made products cannot be returned, exchanged, or refunded once production has started unless the item is received damaged, defective, or incorrect.

Damaged or Incorrect Products

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or you receive the wrong product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery.

To help us resolve the issue quickly, please provide:

  • Your Order Number
  • Clear photos of the product
  • Photos of the packaging (if damaged)

Refunds

Once your request is reviewed and approved, we will either:

  • Send a replacement product, or
  • Issue a refund to your original payment method (where applicable).

Cancellation Policy

Orders may only be cancelled before production begins. Once printing or customization has started, cancellation requests cannot be accepted.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Personalized products
  • Products damaged due to misuse
  • Products with minor color variations caused by screen settings
